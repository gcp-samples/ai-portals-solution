How Agents Use ARD

AI agents (like Gemini, Claude, or customized enterprise assistants) fetch this manifest from /.well-known/ai-catalog.json to:

  • Identify what capabilities are hosted on your domain.
  • Understand what protocols (MCP vs REST) are required.
  • Select the correct tools dynamically based on natural language task descriptions.
